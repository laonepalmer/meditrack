Our Idea

Why We Built MediTrack

MediTrack is a student project designed to help people living with diabetes access simple, clear, and trustworthy information about diabetes, medication safety, adherence tips, and reliable health resources.

📘

Education

We explain diabetes in simple language so users can understand the condition better.

💊

Medication Safety

We provide reminders about safe medication habits and encourage users to follow professional advice.

💚

Healthy Habits

We share practical tips that can help users stay consistent and build healthier routines.

⚠️ This website is for educational purposes only. Always consult a healthcare professional.
