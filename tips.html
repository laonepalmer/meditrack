Stay Consistent, Stay Healthy

No daily medication checklist here — just practical adherence tips.

Helpful Tips

  • ⏰
    Set daily reminders.

    Use phone alarms or calendar reminders to reduce forgetfulness.

  • 📍
    Keep medication visible.

    Place it somewhere safe but easy to remember, away from children.

  • 🍽️
    Link medicine to a routine.

    Take medicine with a regular habit, such as breakfast, if allowed by your doctor.

  • 🧳
    Plan when travelling.

    Carry enough medication and keep prescriptions or clinic notes when needed.

  • 📈
    Track your progress.

    Record blood sugar, symptoms, and questions for your next clinic visit.

Source: CDC – Living with Diabetes
